This Aduio SPY device uses GSM modem and Arduino to record the Audio in the surroundings.
GSM modem uses a sim card and Arduino Is programmed to Automatically pick up the phone after 15 sec i.e, 3 rings, whenever the mobile number used in GSM is called.
Connect the RX pin of SIM 800l to pin 10 of Arduino Micro and TX to pin 11.
Then connect the SIM 800l MIC N pin to the negative pin of the microphone sensor and SIM 800I MIC P to the microphoneâ€™s positive pin.
After finishing all connections, power the device either with USB or with a
4 to 5V battery. 
Then deploy the device near a person, which you want to
spy on. 
Now, make a call on the SIM800l. It will be automatically received and transfer of the voice signal to your phone will begin.
